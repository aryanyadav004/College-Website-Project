About Course:-

  • The department of mathematics is one of the pioneer department of this college established in 1957.
  • The department runs several traditional courses in Mathematics for undergraduate as well as postgraduate programme for students pursuing B.Sc.(Hons.), B.A. Math (Hons.), M.Sc. (Maths).
  • The department offers opportunities for education and research in a wide spectrum of areas in Mathematics such as: Algebra, Analysis, Differential Equations, Discrete Mathematics, Operations Research, Mathematical Modelling etc.
